Import and Export

PAN INDUS EXIM has a wide range of trade products and we wish that our customers are content with our services. Some of our trade products would be Machinery, Machine spares, Raw Material (Iron & Steel Bars/Rods etc.), Spare parts for automobiles in Rubber & metal bounded, Silicon base Pharmaceutical spares, Food & Beverage, Dairy, Bakery, Cosmetic and Engineering Segments, Pipe and Pipe fittings, Cashew nuts, Pepper, Turmeric, Banana, Kokum / Kokum Syrup, Kurtis and Salwars, Wooden Toys and many more products.

PAN INDUS EXIM procures these from selective producers for sale in the international market. As our core values are integrity and customer centricity, we ensure that our organization is committed in providing the products of standards promised by us and see that our approach is customer centric.

Quality


Pan Indus Exim believes in consistency and transparency in every dealing by providing best quality products to our international customers to meet their requirements. We are associated with reputed manufacturers who are best in the industry and have proven record of their qualities. Each and every product is packed under the supervision of our team. Pan Indus Exim’s foremost endeavor is to provide the best products and services to our valuable customers and to make them delighted in every transaction from start to end. We have a dedicated team of qualified technicians and supervisors who ensure high quality products by enquiring, close monitoring on every stage of production, Inspection, packing etc. We also provide thirty party inspection of the goods in case desired by the customer. Along with that we have an established and credible tie-up with different manufacturers who are pioneers in this field thus 100% genuineness of the products is guaranteed. All our products come with the required quality assurance certificate.
